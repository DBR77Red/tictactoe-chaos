04 — Power Cards

Card System

Each player draws 3 random cards at game start. Cards have a unique neon glow color, SVG icon, effect description, and flavor copy. Hover to feel the lift.

SPAWN BOARD
Add an adjacent 3×3 board to the battlefield. 1× per game
"Expand the battlefield — your opponent won't see it coming."
ERASE
Remove one opponent mark from anywhere on the board.
"What once was theirs is now nothing. Clean the slate."
9 GRID
Transforms the match into Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe — 9 boards within a board.
"Go deeper. Win the board to win the board."
MIRROR STRIKE
Steal one opponent mark and convert it to your symbol.
"Their move becomes your weapon."
FREEZE
Lock one row or column — unplayable for 1 full round.
"Time stops — for them."
DOUBLE DOWN
Place 2 marks anywhere valid in a single turn.
"One move wasn't enough. Take two."
TIME WARP
Undo the last 2 moves — one from each player.
"Rewrite history. One moment at a time."
